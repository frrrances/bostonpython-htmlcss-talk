Raspberry Lemonade*

prep:
15 mins
total time:
45 mins
servings:
12

Ingredients

  • 10 sliced lemons
  • 2 cups raspberries
  • 1 1/2 cups sugar
  • 6 cups water
  • 2 1/4 cups tequila blanco (optional)
  • Mint, for garnish

Directions

  1. Combine lemons, raspberries, and sugar in a large pot. Pound the mixture firmly with the end of a straight rolling pin (or a large wooden spoon), extracting as much juice as possible, about 10 minutes.
  2. Stir in water. Pour through a sieve into a large bowl. Press the solids until all juice is extracted. Discard the solids. Serve over ice.
  3. Cocktail version: Stir tequila into lemonade. Serve over ice, and garnish with mint.

Source: Everyday Food, May 2009

*with a kick
